About

Shayne is a self-taught chef who has been cooking and baking for her family for nearly 40 years. Often replicated, never quite duplicated, despite her daughter's best attempts, Shayne's expertise is second-to-none.

The recipes collected here are favorites and staples, with a primarily Jewish influence. Some are originals and some are Shayne's doctored-up version of her mother's creations.
